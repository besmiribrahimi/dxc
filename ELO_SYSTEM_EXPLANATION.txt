ASCEND ENTRENCHED: ELO SYSTEM EXPLANATION
==========================================

The platform has moved to a new ELO-centric ranking system to better
represent skill, consistency, and match performance.

HOW IT WORKS:
-------------
1. STARTING POINT: Every operator starts with a base ELO of 1000.
2. MATCH RESULTS: ELO increases when you win matches and decreases when you lose.
3. QUALITY OF OPPONENT: Beating a high-ELO team gives more points than 
   beating a low-ELO team.
4. WIN RATE MATTERS: Your overall Win/Loss ratio now directly impacts your 
   Global Influence score.

THE "INFLUENCE" FORMULA:
------------------------
Your position on the leaderboard is determined by an "Influence Score":
(ELO × 0.6) + (Win Rate × 200) + Rank Bonus

- ELO (60% weight): Your raw skill ceiling and match history.
- Win Rate: Rewards consistency and team play.
- Rank Bonus: Extra weight given to top-performing sector veterans.

WHY THE CHANGE?
---------------
The old Level/KD system didn't account for team play or the difficulty 
of opponents. The ELO system ensures that "Grinding" alone isn't enough; 
you must win against quality teams to climb the ranks.

Stay sharp, Operator. See you on the battlefield.
