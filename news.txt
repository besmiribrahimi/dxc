# TSC-Assisted RKA 2 defeats IA in Champagne

Moments ago, Krakozhia 2 (RKA 2), with the assistance of  The Southern Calamity (TSC) fought Imperio Aleman (IA) in Champagne. A 17v17, RKA 2 was Central while IA was Entente. During the battle, RKA 2+TSC managed to easily hold C3 against IA and subsequently pushed into E3 during their first attack phase, capturing it after four minutes of fighting. For the rest of the game, IA managed to hold onto E2, despite some heavy pushes by RKA 2 and TSC. 

The result was a 4-2 victory for RKA 2 and TSC, with the final casualties being 682 for IA and 520 for RKA 2. This battle was controversial as IA had only agreed to fight RKA 2, with it only being apparent that TSC would be involved right as the battle started. Furthermore, after the battle, IA accused one of RKA 2's players of cheating, while RKA 2 denied this, they did agree to do a PC check.

According to an ET investigation, TSC's presence was around 9 out of the 17 member roster, making it impossible to measure RKA 2's individual skill, as they seemed to have been carried by TSC. TSC is the successor of The Teutonic Imperium 2 (TTI 2) and was formed after the closure of the 271st, consisting of members from both factions. Going forward, TSC may play a larger role in the community as their players already have a track record of being elite.

# TWA and IA Stalemate in Champagne

Yesterday, The White Army (TWA) fought Imperio Aleman (IA) in Champagne. A 15v15, TWA was Entente while IA was Central. During the battle, both sides struggled significantly against each other, and managed consistently to enter each others point in large numbers, however were ultimately always pushed out. While there were some periods one side significantly overwhelmed the other, it never resulted in even a foothold, maintaining a relatively balanced back and fourth throughout.

The result was a 3-3 stalemate, with the final casualties being 611 for TWA and 697 for IA. The battle itself was relatively controversial, as TWA accused IA of intentionally kicking their best player, however IA claimed they were an alt account, which TWA denied. The result does demonstrate that TWA's skill capacity is rising, the EU-majority faction's ability to stalemate a South American clan in roughly equal ping showcases they are now on the level of IA, a top-mid skill faction. While for IA it was not the best result, it does not signify any decrease in skill.

# NDV DESTROYS DSA in Champagne

An hour ago, Nordoslav (NDV) fought DSA in Champagne. A 16v16, NDV was Central while Deutsche Schwarze Armee (DSA, also known as Agartha) was Entente. During the battle, NDV managed to completely outcompete DSA, capturing E3 within 2 and a half minutes of their first attack. While DSA managed to hold the defense-favoured E2 for one attack phase, NDV broke through in their 2nd phase, capturing the point in under 3 minutes. While for E1, NDV managed to enter the point after 6 minutes of heavy fighting, eventually capturing it.

The result was a 6-0 victory for NDV, with the final casualties being 554 for DSA and 355 for NDV. There was some controversy, during the battle, NDV accused DSA of attempting a blatant canyon, as they had uncovered a continuous chasm to the bottom of the map along the E1's boundary. While DSA initially denied this, they later stated "screw the rules". This result shows a huge skill gap between NDV and DSA, while expected, its extent was surprising. DSA is a relatively unknown faction that has seen controversy due to its leader being the suspected alt of a hacker, with several other members being accused of hacking as well.
