Competition Specifications

Draft Format

18 Factions split into 3 Divisions. Each Division contains 2 Groups of 3 Factions.

Match Protocol

5-6 weeks of engagement. Frontlines mode, single round per battle. Schedule is flexible between faction leaders.

Promotion & Relegation

1st place promoted. 2nd place stays. 3rd place relegated. Div 3 relegation results in potential replacement by candidate factions.

The Grand Final

Top factions from Division 1 face off in a seasonal climax to crown the EPL Champion.
